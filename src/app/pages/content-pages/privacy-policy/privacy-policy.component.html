Introduction

Welcome to RareScope! We prioritize your privacy and are dedicated to protecting your personal information. This policy details our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection as you engage with the RareScope platform

Information We Collect
  • Patient contact group information: We collect your email address, patient organization and country when you register an account or interact with our services.
  • Patient health group information: We collect health information specific to your patient group in a consolidated manner (at group level), not at an individual patient level.
How We Use Your Information
  • To manage and maintain your user account.
  • To communicate with you about platform updates and to offer support.
  • To facilitate your participation in the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure regulatory compliance.
Data Management and Storage
  • Only your email, patient organization, country and the list of items associated with your organiation are stored by RareScope.
  • The data collected is stored securely in our cloud storage.
Sharing of Your Information
  • We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer this information to outside parties.
  • The anonymous medical information collected by RareScope will be shared only with Eurordis and EMA.
Your Rights Under GDPR
  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of the data provided to us.
  • Right to Rectification: You may ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of the data provided to us.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: At any time, you may withdraw your consent for us to use your data.
Consent
  • By using our platform, you consent to our privacy policy.
Changes to Our Privacy Policy
  • We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date of this policy. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed.
